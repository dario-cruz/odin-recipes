Recipe:Thor's ThunderCakes!

Thor is making them cakes clap, ThunderClap!

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Vegi Oil
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Eggs
  • Icing

Instructions:

  • Blend together all ingrediants into a large mixing bowl.
  • Take your hammer and smash the bowl, plus all the ingredients.
  • Step back and let the dust clear, there should be 4x cupcakes on the floor right now.
