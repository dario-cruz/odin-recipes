Recipe:Loki's Latkes!

These potato bites are full of shenanigans!

Ingredients:

  • Russet Potatoes
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • Canola Oil
  • Chives (Chopped)
  • Applesauce (for serving)
  • Sour Cream (for serving)

Instructions:

  • Grate potatoes and transfer into bowl with ice water. Squeeze out as much liquid and let rest.
  • In large bowl combine potatoes, eggs, flour, and some salt.
  • Heat oil in a large pan. Test pan temp before starting to cook.
  • Add spoonful of potato mix to pan and press down to flatten. Fry until crispy.
  • Serve with chives, applesauce, and sour cream.
